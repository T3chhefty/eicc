About EICC

Founded on July 4, 2021, the Excel International Christian Center (EICC) is a vibrant and growing church committed to nurturing believers who thrive in every facet of life. Our foundation is built upon the powerful combination of God's infallible Word and the transformative presence of the Holy Spirit.

We are located at #3 Ukpibo Street, behind PPL Umutu, Ukwuani LGA, Delta State, Nigeria. We extend a warm and heartfelt invitation to everyone to join our fellowship and experience the tangible presence of God in a community where faith flourishes.

Meet Our Senior Pastor

Pastor Mike Edmond Otali

Our esteemed Senior Pastor, General Overseer, and Founder, Pastor Mike Edmond Otali, is a passionate servant of God. He is dedicated to teaching the unadulterated truth of the Gospel and empowering individuals to discover and fulfill their divine purpose. His ministry is characterized by a strong emphasis on fervent prayer, the transformative power of the Word, and fostering a loving community where faith can grow and people can experience God's miraculous touch.
