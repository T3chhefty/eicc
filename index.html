About EICC

Excel International Christian Center (EICC) was founded on 4th July 2021 with a vision to raise a generation of believers who excel in every area of life through the power of God's Word and Spirit.

We are located at: #3 Ukpibo Street, behind PPL Umutu, Ukwuani LGA, Delta State, Nigeria. We invite you to join us and experience the warmth of our fellowship and the transforming power of God's presence.

Meet Our Senior Pastor

Our General Overseer, Founder, and Senior Pastor is Pastor Mike Edmond Otali.

Pastor Mike is a passionate servant of God, dedicated to teaching the unadulterated truth of the Gospel and raising a generation of believers who walk in dominion. His ministry is marked by a deep commitment to prayer, the Word, and empowering individuals to fulfill their divine purpose.

He believes in the transformative power of God's love and is committed to fostering a vibrant community where everyone can grow in faith and experience God's miraculous touch.
